Knob Hills Trail Race

10k - 25k - 50k - 100k

Join us for the third annual Knob Hills Trail Race on the challenging DORBA Knob Hills Trail. This looped course will take racers along the westerly side of Lake Grapevine and along Denton Creek through some of the grittiest trail in North Texas.

Join us for the second annual Knob Hills Trail Race on the challenging DORBA Knob Hills Trail. This looped course will take racers along the westerly side of Lake Grapevine and along Denton Creek through some of the grittiest trail in North Texas. Start/Finish is at Scout Barn 771 off of Dunham Road. There is ample parking and amenities are available at the Start/Finish and the opposite end of the loop on 377 just south of 1171. Gender specific shirts available, however the women's sizes run SMALL. All registrations after January 9 will receive unisex shirt. All finishers will receive a finisher award. Additional awards will be given to M 1-3, F 1-3 and Masters (45+) M&F 1-3.
